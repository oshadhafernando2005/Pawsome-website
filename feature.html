Basic Features
Some of the basic features that the app consists.
  • Secure user authentication
  • User-friendly interface
  • Data privacy and Security
  • Seamless integration and performance
AI Chatbot
Creating a virtual assistant for pet care support, offering guidance on diet and hygiene management as well as providing a minor health issue analysis for preliminary pet health concerns.
Community Feature
Develop a social network for pet owners to connect, share resources and provide mutual support as well as supplying functions for organizing pet events, searching for lost pets and helping stray animals. Moreover, pet owners can help other pet owners by pet sitting their pets when they are away through this community.
